Name : Vegetarian Quinoa Bowl

Calories: 380 cal

Ingredients:

1. 1 cup cooked quinoa

2. Roasted vegetables (bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes)

3. Chickpeas, rinsed and drained

4. Feta cheese, crumbled

5. Lemon-tahini dressing

Instructions:

In a bowl, layer cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and crumbled feta cheese. Drizzle with lemon-tahini dressing and toss before serving.", calories: 380
