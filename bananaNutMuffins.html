Name : Banana Nut Muffins

Calories: 240 cal

Ingredients:

1. 1 cup all-purpose flour

2. 1/2 cup sugar

3. 1 teaspoon baking powder

4. 1/2 teaspoon baking soda

5. 1/4 teaspoon salt

5. 2 ripe bananas, mashed

5. 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted

5. 1/4 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 350Â°F (175Â°C). In a bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. In another bowl, mix mashed bananas and melted butter. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in chopped nuts. Spoon the batter into muffin cups. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.
