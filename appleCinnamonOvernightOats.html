Name : Apple Cinnamon Overnight Oats

Calories: 320 cal

Ingredients:

1. 1/2 cup rolled oats

2. 1/2 cup Greek yogurt

3. 1/2 cup almond milk

4. 1 apple, diced

5. 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

6. 1 tablespoon honey

Instructions:

In a jar, combine rolled oats, Greek yogurt, almond milk, diced apple, and cinnamon. Stir well. Refrigerate overnight. In the morning, drizzle honey on top and enjoy.", calories: 290
