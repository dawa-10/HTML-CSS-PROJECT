Name : Spinach and Feta Omelette

Calories: 320 cal

Ingredients:

1. 3 eggs, beaten

2. 1/4 cup fresh spinach,chopped

3. 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled

4. 1 tablespoon olive oil

5. Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

In a pan, sautÃ© fresh spinach in olive oil until wilted. Pour beaten eggs over the spinach. Sprinkle feta cheese on top. Cook until the eggs are set. Season with salt and pepper. Fold the omelette and serve.
