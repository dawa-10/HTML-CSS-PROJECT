Breakfast Recipes

Name : Avocado and Tomato Toast

Calories: 280 cal

Ingredients:

1. 2 eggs

2. 1 avocado, mashed

3. 2 slices of whole-grain bread, toasted

4. Salt and pepper to taste

5. Chopped chives for garnish

Instructions:

Place sliced avocado and tomato on toasted bread. Season with salt and pepper. Garnish with chopped cilantro.
